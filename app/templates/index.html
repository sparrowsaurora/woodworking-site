Some Common FAQs

And some that T want to share

Do you take custom orders?

Yes! We welcome custom projects. You can share your designs, sketches, or ideas, and we'll work with you to create something unique to your needs and style.

Can I provide my own design or inspiration photos?

Absolutely. We're happy to work from your inspiration images, sketches, or architectural plans. We'll help translate your ideas into a practical woodworking solution.

How long does a custom project take?

Timelines vary based on the size and complexity of the project. Most small projects take 2-4 weeks, while larger ones (like full furniture sets or built-ins) may take 6-10 weeks.

What is your pricing like?

Prices depend on the size, design, materials, and finish. We offer quotes after an initial consultation. Custom work is priced to reflect craftsmanship and material quality.

How do I get started with a project?

Just contact us through our website or call us directly. We'll set up a consultation to discuss your project, provide a quote, and outline the next steps.
